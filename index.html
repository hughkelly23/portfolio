Profile

Edinburgh-based problem solver and design enthusiast focused on finding clear, useful and creative solutions for complex problems.


It's nice to meet you!


I spent a large chunk of my 20's working in the music industry. This gave me a lot of interesting experiences and stories, from jamming with Bill Murray and accidentally getting him involved in a tabloid scandal (sorry Bill), to winning Best Original Score at the Berlin Independent Film Festival (which I still haven't collected, eek).


Eventually, I wanted to channel that same creativity into solving practical problems for people. UX and Product Design turned out to be the right fit, and I haven’t looked back. Drop me a wee email to say hello if you would like to chat more!
